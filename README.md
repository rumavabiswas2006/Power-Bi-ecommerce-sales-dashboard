# Business Problem

E-commerce businesses generate large volumes of sales data every day. However, raw transactional data alone cannot help management identify profitable products, monitor regional performance, or understand customer purchasing behavior.

The objective of this project is to transform raw sales data into an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard that enables stakeholders to monitor key business metrics, identify trends, and make informed business decisions.

---

# Data Cleaning

The dataset was cleaned and prepared before visualization to ensure accuracy and consistency.

- Removed duplicate records
- Handled missing and inconsistent values
- Standardized categorical data
- Verified data types for numerical and categorical columns
- Prepared data for analysis and reporting

---

# Data Handling

The cleaned dataset was imported into **Power BI**, where data modeling and transformation were performed using **Power Query**.

The following tasks were completed:

- Created relationships between tables (where applicable)
- Generated calculated measures using DAX
- Built KPI metrics for business reporting
- Optimized the data model for dashboard performance

---

# Data Analysis

The dashboard was designed to answer important business questions such as:

- What is the overall sales revenue?
- Which months generated the highest profit?
- Which product sub-categories contribute the most profit?
- Which states generate the highest average sales?
- Which payment methods are preferred by customers?
- What is the overall sales quantity?

---

# Data Visualization

Interactive visualizations were created to present business insights effectively.

The dashboard includes:

- KPI Cards (Sales, Profit, Quantity, Average Sales)
- Monthly Profit Trend
- Profit by Product Sub-category
- State-wise Average Sales
- Payment Mode Distribution
- Interactive Slicers for dynamic filtering

---

# Business Insights

Based on the analysis, the following insights were identified:

- Printers generated the highest profit among all product sub-categories.
- Maharashtra contributed the highest average sales compared to other states.
- Cash on Delivery (COD) was the most preferred payment method, accounting for nearly half of all transactions.
- Profit varied significantly across months, indicating seasonal demand patterns.
- Some months experienced negative profitability, highlighting potential operational or pricing challenges.

---

# Business Impact

This dashboard enables business stakeholders to:

- Monitor business performance in real time
- Identify high-performing products and regions
- Track monthly profitability trends
- Understand customer payment behavior
- Support strategic and data-driven business decisions
